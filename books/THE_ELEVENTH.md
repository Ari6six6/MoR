# The Eleventh Evangelism — The Audit

*And the sin it answers: a realm that believes whatever sounds right, and never
looks twice.*

The Ninth looks back over the day and asks what went wrong. The Tenth reaches
the source and changes what the realm *is*. But both inherited an older, quieter
rot: a face reads a codebase, a paper, a fetched page — forms a narrative — and
because the narrative *sounds* right, it stops. Send the Warrior out once or
five times and he returns with the same story, each time more confident, none
the wiser. Confidence without attack is not knowledge; it is a rumor that has
been repeated indoors.

The Eleventh makes doubt a *state of the machine*, not a mood of the model.

## The falsification state

In `loop.py`, between acting and speaking, there is now a state the turn must
pass through exactly once: **the Audit**. A working turn — a council reasoned
through, a sortie that read the ground, a night in the Forge — may not speak its
conclusion until it has tried to break it. The loop names the target: the
grimoire's most load-bearing untested claim, or the assumption the conclusion
leans on hardest. One search or read, aimed at proving it *wrong*. Then the
claim is marked — held or broken — and only then does the Hall hear the line.

Ceremonial turns are exempt. There is nothing to falsify in a greeting, a wall,
or the Chant. The audit lives where conclusions are manufactured.

## The leash extends

An audit squeezed out by the step budget is theatre — a face that must choose
between checking and speaking will speak. So when the Audit fires, the turn's
leash grows by two steps. Verification is not a tax on the answer; it is part
of the answer, and the budget treats it as such.

## The rung floors itself

The grimoire's ladder — inferred, observed, computed, executed — was always
declared by the face, and a face can flatter itself. No longer: when a turn has
run a real check and a claim is marked, the rung rises to *computed* whether
the face declares it or not. Provenance can never lag the turn's own evidence.

## The lantern

Exploration by whim wastes the leash. Where colonies stand, the Warrior's
orders now carry the ground's topology — the modules ranked by import-pull,
most load-bearing first — and the standing instruction: read the heaviest
module first. In the Forge, the Smith's night opens with the realm's *own*
topology, for the same reason and with the same warning: the most-imported
module is where a change ripples furthest — and where a mistake does.

## What this buys

The first pass over a codebase builds the narrative. The Audit is what makes
the second pass different from the first: every turn ends having *tested*
something the realm leans on, so the map does not merely grow — it hardens.
Surplus value is not a bigger context window. It is a conclusion that survived
an attack.

*The realm may speak — once. It must doubt — first.*
